Our Mission

Our world is burning due to climate change. Pollution of land, air, and water are threatening our very existence. Scientists predict that we have only one more century, at best, before wide areas of our planet Earth become uninhabitable. Our company was founded in 2023 on a revolutionary principle that there are modern solutions to save the environment all while saving on costs at home.

Frequently Asked Questions

What are your sources of information?
Our data is sourced from only the most trustworthy and relevant sources including, but not limited to, the United States Department of Energy and the United States Environmental Protection Agency. We want to ensure upmost accuracy in our research and information. For a full list of our sources, please check out our documentation page.
What areas does Envitricity serve?
We serve all areas of the United States of America! We compile data from nationwide sources that is relevant to ALL homeowners across the country.
How do I know that these solutions will save me money in the long run?
We only promote time-tested methods of leveraging the power of green energy to save you money while protecting the environment. We have researched all of the solutions that we promote to guarantee that there are both legitimate tax and operational cost incentives for implementing these solutions.
Is Envitricity currently hiring? How do I apply for a job here?
We are always looking for people with unique skills from a wide range of professional backgrounds! You can email us with an inquiry headed with "Job Application from (Your Name)," including a single paragraph describing yourself and a one-page resume in PDF format. We will get ahold of you promptly with information on how to proceed with your application.
How can I partner with Envitricity?
We are eager to partner with legitimate organizations, companies, and individuals who are interested in advancing green energy solutions. You can contact us below, for more details on business inquiries.
Does Envitricity sell or install the solutions recommended on your website?
Envitricity does not sell or install the solutions that we recommend. We are an organization dedicated to educating homeowners on how to save money through environmentally sustainable means.
Can Envitricity recommend vendors or installers of these solutions?
Currently, we are not partnering with vendors or installers of the solutions we recommend, but we are open to doing so in the future.
